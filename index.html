Welcome to Flowers for Weddings in Spain, your English Florist on the Costa Blanca. Flowers for Weddings in Spain is brought to you by Diamond Flores Spain, the number one English florist on the southern Costa Blanca, in Los Montesinos, which is close to Rojales, Guardamar, Torrevieja and Alicante where you can also order flowers for all other occasions either by visiting our shop or by using our other website www.diamondfloresspain.com

 

Our gallery page shows a selection of the wedding flower arrangements previously supplied by us, which we hope will provide you with some inspiration. However, we appreciate that your wedding day is your special dayandwe know that you will want to choose designs, flowers and colours yourself. Please do not hesitate to contact us so that we may discuss your requirements fully. We are confident that we will be able to provide you with what ever you need within your required budget.

 

We can also arrange other services including Wedding Planning, to help you to find venues, photography, video, dresses, cakes, cars and much more. If you require a Wedding Planner to help you with all the services just send us an email to royanna2225@hotmail.com  We look forward to hearing from you to help make your dream wedding in Spain become a reality!
